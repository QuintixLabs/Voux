Here's the quick version: you run your own copy of Voux, so you control what it does and you're responsible for it.

1. You're in charge

When you host Voux, it sits on your server. Make sure it follows the laws where you live, especially around privacy and data retention.

2. Your counters, your data

All counters and hits stay in your SQLite file. Nothing gets sent back to us. Back it up if the stats matter to you.

3. Keep it reasonable

Don't use Voux to collect more data than you need. It only dedupes by IP. If you don't want to keep those hits, clear them out.

Voux is free software with no warranty. If something breaks, you're responsible for fixing it.
